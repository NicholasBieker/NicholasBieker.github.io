Penguins of Madagascar

Kowalski, analysis - Skipper

Madagascar is known for its penguin population due to the Dreamworks documentary The Penguins of Madagascar. There, a group of wildlife fotographers films the actions of four penguins with the following names:

  • Skipper
  • Kowalsky
  • Rico
  • Private

Skipper is the leader of the group, Kowalski is the brains, Rico is the muscle, and Private is the heart. Most of the action takes place in the Central Park Zoo where the group performs various paramilitary missions to protect their home.
